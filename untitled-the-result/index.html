Untitled: The result

2016

2016

 [ Untitled: The result ]

recycled E-waste, FRP structure, lcd module, energy harvesting module, xeon light, moss, agar, carbon fabric, custom electronic circuit

 size variable
 “ Light <-> Light ”

Brief :

[ Untitled: The result ] is an installation that transferring bio-energy into electronic current. The installation will trigger out Xeon flash light in a period which due to how the oxidation effects active in those chambers. Each trigger will exhaust all of power that harvested. Artist is trying to present the procedure of energy transformation and transformation and expressing natural light -> artificial light, and the difficulty of energy management.
