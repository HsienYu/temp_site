HsienYu Cheng

chenghsienyu (1)

1984 born in Kaohsiung, Taiwan. Artist & Software developer based in Taipei now.Most of HsienYu’s works are electronic installations, software, experimental bioelectronics devices. His works are concerned with the relationship between human behavior, emotion, software and machine. He tries to bring out the meaning of life through his works that are filled with his own observation and feelings toward society & environment in a humorous way.Currently he is focusing on field of biology, electronic, software and making tools for creative industrial application. Selected by Dutch YoungTalent, and First Prize of Taipei Digital Art Award, Quality Award of New Media of Kaohsiung Art Award, and Tung Chung Art Award of Hong’s Foundation.
